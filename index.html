ABOUT ME

Xiang Zhang

Xiang is currently a Ph.D. student (since 2016) at School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales (UNSW), Australia. He is supervised by Dr. Lina Yao in Data Dynamics (D2) lab.

Xiang received his Master degree (in 2016) from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), China. His research interests lay in data mining, deep learning, and reinforcement learning with focusing applications on Brain-Computer Interface (BCI), health care (e.g., neurological disorder diagnosis), and human activity recognition.
